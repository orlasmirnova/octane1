Meet Orla

I realised that I had a passion for makeup when I was fifteen years old. Unfortunately, I didn't have the right tools so I looked a complete mess! This is when I realised that the world needs affordable makeup brushes that don't skimp on quality and are ethical too. I have tested my line of brushes countless times to make sure that they work perfectly straight out of the box. I have worked with a team of artists to create a pastel colour scheme to introduce elegance and a sense of calm to every vanity table. I have been running Orla's Brushes since 2015 and I feel like I have the best job in the world. Our HQ is based by the seaside in Brighton, UK. Every colloboration presents a new challenge and no two days are the same. Outside of the beauty industry, I am a passionate oil painter who is obsessed with her cat (Darcy)
